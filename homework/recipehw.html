how to cook an omelette

This is a step by step guide on how to make the perfect omelette

This recipe should take you roughly 5 minutes to cook, follow it step by step and you will have the perfect omelette every time!

Recipe:

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tsb sunflower oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper

Step by step:

  1. Step 1:

    season the beaten eggs with salt and pepper and preheat the non stick pan with the sunflower oil

  2. Step 2:

    once heated pour the eggs into the pan and sway it from side to side until the mixture has covered the pan completely

  3. Step 3:

    once cooked a little you can add whatever you like - some grated cheese, vegetables etc. when done cooking serve onto a plate and enjoy.
